PULSE OPENER

Title sequence for a European design festival — kinetic typography, geometric abstraction, and rhythm-driven motion choreography.

// YEAR2024
// ROLEMOTION DESIGNER
// TOOLSAFTER EFFECTS · C4D
// DURATION1 MIN 47 SEC

// 01 — OVERVIEW

Pulse Opener was commissioned as the main title sequence for a design festival held in Prague. The brief was deliberately open: create something that communicates energy, precision, and the intersection of craft and technology.

The sequence is built around a single typographic motif — the festival name — that fractures, reassembles, and morphs across 107 seconds. Sound sync was handled frame-accurately, with each beat triggering a geometric transformation.

Rendered in 4K and adapted into three format variants: cinema widescreen, square social loop, and vertical mobile cut.

// 03 — PROCESS

STEP 01 STORYBOARD 60-frame thumbnail storyboard drawn across three pacing options. Client selected the rhythm-driven version.
STEP 02 ANIMATIC Lo-fi animatic built in After Effects with placeholder type and temp audio. Beat-locked at 128 BPM to match the festival's sound direction.
STEP 03 3D GEOMETRY Geometric elements modelled and animated in Cinema 4D, composited back in After Effects via EXR sequences.
STEP 04 FORMAT CUT Three format variants cut and re-paced for cinema, square social, and vertical mobile. Delivered in ProRes 4444.
