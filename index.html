ABOUT

MARTIN PIREK GRAPHIC DESIGNER

I'm a brand designer and visual communicator working at the intersection of strategy and craft. I help emerging brands find their visual language — from the first logo to the last printed page.

My background is rooted in philosophy, which shapes how I approach design: not as decoration, but as a way of making meaning visible. Good design, to me, is always an argument — one made through form, type, and image rather than words.

I work across brand identity, print, and digital — with a particular interest in brands that have something genuine to say. If you're building something and you're not sure yet how it should look, that's usually where I'm most useful.
